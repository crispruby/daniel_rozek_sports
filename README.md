# Daniel Rozek — Sports & Fitness Profile
This repository contains the source code for my personal athletics website, which highlights my journey in sports, fitness, and long‑distance training. It serves as a dedicated space to share my active lifestyle, multi‑sport background, and ongoing commitment to staying healthy and fit.

👉 You can view the [**Live Web**](https://crispruby.github.io/daniel_rozek_sports/about/)

## About This Site 🏃‍♂️
This athletics profile includes:

- My background as a retired Black Belt of Tae-Kwon-Do
- My ongoing involvement in cross‑country skiing, cycling, hiking, running, and soccer
- My approach to staying active and maintaining long‑term fitness
- Personal reflections on training, discipline, and athletic growth
- Photos and content used for sports‑related introductions or submissions
- The purpose of this site is to share my athletic journey and active lifestyle in a clean, simple, and focused format.

## Why This Site Exists 🌄
This repository provides a dedicated home for my sports and fitness story.
It keeps my athletic content organized and easy to share with people who are interested specifically in my fitness background.

## Technology Used 🛠
This site is built using:

- GitHub Pages
- Jekyll
- Minimal Mistakes theme
